Kaveman Bar & Grill was established by Kyle in 2008 and is owned by him & his wife Emily. They have two wonderful little boys, Charles and Benjamin. Kyle and Emily have created a very welcoming environment for all. Their menu has been curated with old family recipes that have been passed on, as well as the delicious meals they have discovered through their travels.
